Events

  • Date : July 17th 2012

    Come spend the evening with us. Enjoy a guided tour around the zoo at dusk / night to see how the animals come alive when the sun sets.

    Many of our animals get fed in the evening after most people have gone home, so you'll get a chance to see some sight most don't.

    Tickets for this event are limited, so book early - prices are £22 (Adult) £17 (Kids).

  • Date : August 2012

    During August we will be running a treasure hunt around the zoo. Come and see if you can find the gold!

    Pick up a treasure map on your way in and the follow the clues dotted across the site.

    Collect all the clues and recieve a special gift from the zoo shop as your bounty!

  • Date : October 2012

    Work starts on the new Elephant enclosure in October. The existing enclosure has been open since 1983 and is getting too small now.

    The new enclosure will be 3 times the size of the current one and once complete will be the largest enclosure in Europe.

    The old enclosure will eventually house an african exhibit - more news to come!

    Whilst these essential works are carried out, we will be open, but certain areas will be closed to the public.

Ticket Office hours 07:00 - 17:30
