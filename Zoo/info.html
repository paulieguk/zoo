Visitors Info

Animal Kingdom Zoo
Burton Road
Kings Wallop
Bedfordshire
BF4 6TX

  • Food and Drink

    We offer great value for money food for both children and adults with a fantastic selection of delicious food including; jacket potatoes, sandwiches and cakes all from local producers. We also offer special facilities for pre-booked groups.

    Picnic Areas
    There are several open grassy areas and picnic benches within the zoo which you are very welcome to picnic on.

  • Disabled Facilities

    Cafés, toilets and most animal houses are accessible by wheelchair. Seeing eye dogs are permitted at the zoo, although there is no access to the apes and elephants. There is no disabled access to the inside of the lemur house.

    Mobility Scooters

    Mobility scooters are available for hire at £12.00 (to be booked in advance) from Guest Services. Identification should be produced on arrival.

  • Toilet Facilities

    There are several toilet blocks within the zoo, including disabled toilets and those with baby changing facilities. Do visit the Himalaya bathrooms to see the amazing leaf cutter ants!

    All toilet waste is collected on site at the zoo, processed and used with the zoo animal waste as fertiliser for the tropical plants.
